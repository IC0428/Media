India

Location

India is a country in South Asia. It's bordered by the Indian Ocean to the south, the Bay of Bengal to the southeast, and the Arabian Sea to the southwest. It also shares land borders with Pakistan, China, Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh, and Myanmar.

Size:

India is the world's seventh-largest country by area. It covers an area of 3,287,263 sq km

Population:

India is the world's most populous country.
